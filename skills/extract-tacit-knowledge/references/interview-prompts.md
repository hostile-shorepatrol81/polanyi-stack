# Interview Prompts

Use these prompts only after the task and scene are narrowly defined.

## Think-Aloud

- Walk through the task in real time. What are you noticing first?
- What signal would make you change direction here?
- What are you ignoring on purpose?
- What would a beginner probably miss at this exact moment?

## Critical Incident

- Tell me about a case where this went unusually well. What did you catch early?
- Tell me about a failure or near miss. What sign was present before the mistake?
- At what point would you stop and escalate instead of continuing?
